Movie Recommendation System

Machine Learning using Python


What is the purpose of this site?
Our website allows user(s) to get recommended movies based on the rating and genre of the movie they searched for. This concept is based on multimedia platforms such as Amazon, Facebook, Netflix and Youtube, who’s recommendation systems help users navigate through the over-abundance of content present online.

Coding Approach:

We did this by training our machine on CSV data from ‘MovieLens’ and 'IMDb', which consisted of over 100,000 user movie ratings and over 10,000 movies with name and genre. We utilised Item-Based Collaborative Filtering in paired with a variety of “Python” libraries to achieve this by training the computer to recognise movies with similar genre and rating and then recommend these to the user. Our machine learning algorithm also draws information from the activities of other users who enjoy similar films (indicated by rating & genre).
